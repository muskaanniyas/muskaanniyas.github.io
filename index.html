About Me

Muskaan Niyas is a dynamic academic scholar, changemaker, and leader whose remarkable achievements reflect her unwavering drive for excellence. A proud student of Arcadia British School, Dubai, Muskaan consistently shines through her leadership, innovation, and dedication to both personal growth and community impact.

Muskaan has embraced various leadership roles, including Student Council Representative, Prefect, and Netball Captain (U19S), earning recognition through honors like the Principal's Award and the title of Netballer of the Year.

As an Academic Mentor at The Tutoring Franchise Corp, she inspires her students, fosters collaboration, and nurtures a culture of academic success across the school.

Her accomplishments extend beyond the classroom and the sports field, showcasing her entrepreneurial mindset and innovative spirit. Through her participation in the Tortoise Tank Business Pitch Competition, Muskaan demonstrated exceptional creativity and problem-solving skills. As a mentor in the BSME DAIGON E-Sports Competition, she further showcased her ability to guide and empower others in challenging environments.

Muskaan's passion for making a difference is evident in her extensive volunteer work. She led the Slam Dunk Nurdle Hunt: NBA Beach Clean-Up Challenge, collaborating with NBA players to combat plastic pollution, and supported initiatives such as the NYUAD Electric Vehicle Grand Prix, the Inclusion Conference, and the Global Wellness Walk at Jubail Mangroves Island.

As an environmental enthusiast and Mental Health Ambassador, Muskaan is committed to promoting sustainability and well-being within her school and beyond. Her advocacy reflects a deep commitment to fostering a positive and inclusive culture. With interests spanning life sciences, the arts, and sustainability, Muskaan exemplifies the qualities of a well-rounded, forward-thinking individual.

Her role as an ISP Changemakers Leader further underscores her passion for driving innovation and creating a better future. Recognized as a role model among her peers, Muskaan is poised to thrive as a global pioneer, blending academic excellence, athletic determination, and visionary leadership to leave a meaningful impact wherever she goes.
